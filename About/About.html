Marc Rozendaal

My name is Marc Rozendaal, I'm 22 and I'm a second year Game Development student at HKU in Utrecht. Most of my time is spent building tools and programming systems, which I'm also incredibly interested in.

I've always been interested in how everything is made, and even more interested in how games are made. I wondered how giant worlds like those in Skyrim and the Witcher could be run on anything other than top of the line hardware. I was intrigued as to how the endless worlds in Minecraft could possibly be generated.

Each time I picked up programming and developing over the years, I was always working on tools and other such things to make life easier down the line. It started out with simple buttons in Processing, and later grew into a simple tile based level editor. During these past two years at HKU I have continued to learn about tool and system programming, and am looking to keep learning as much as I can in the coming years.
